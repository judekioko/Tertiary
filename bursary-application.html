NG-CDF Masinga Bursary Program

The NG-CDF Masinga Bursary Program is designed to support students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds by providing financial assistance for secondary school, college, and university education.

Who Can Apply?

Students enrolled in recognized secondary schools, colleges, or universities.

Applicants must be residents of Masinga Constituency.

How to Apply

All applications must be submitted online through our dedicated bursary application portal. Click the button below to access the bursary application website.
